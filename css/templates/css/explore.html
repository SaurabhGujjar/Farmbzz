Pest

In India about 15-17% of crops are damaged by not taking real time measures against pest and weed.Due to lack of information and negligence pest becomes a major destructive factor to agriculture.Major part of many crops is lost due to presence of pest.It was found in a survey that approximately 30% of Cotton,25% of Rice and 5-10% of Wheat is lost due to this problem. Farmer community faces a great problem in handling the pest present in their farm. Pest a destructive insect or other animal that attacks crops, food,etc.Generally animals are called pests when they attack and cause damage to agriculture by feeding on crops or parasitising livestock, such as boll weevil on cotton.According to International Plant Protection Convention pest is defined as any species, strain or biotype of plant, animal, or pathogenic agent that is injurious to plants or plant products.Sometimes plants may be considered pests themselves i an invasive species.
